We Protect Your Privacy

FalPDF is built with a privacy-first philosophy. Your files and data are yours alone.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

🛡️

Our Core Privacy Commitment

We do not store user files or personal data. Files uploaded to FalPDF are processed in memory and automatically deleted immediately after your download. We never log, analyze, sell, or share your files or any personal information with any third party.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store any files you upload to our servers beyond the processing period
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not require account creation, login, or any form of registration
  • We do not track your file conversion history or usage patterns linked to your identity
  • We do not sell or share any data with third-party advertisers or data brokers
  • We do not use your files to train AI models or for any commercial purpose

File Processing & Deletion

When you upload a file to FalPDF, it is temporarily held in server memory solely for the purpose of processing your conversion request. This process is entirely automated and no human ever views your files.

Upon completion of the conversion (whether successful or not), all uploaded files and output files are permanently deleted from our servers. This deletion occurs automatically and immediately — typically within seconds of your download completing.

Anonymous Usage Analytics

FalPDF may collect basic, anonymous analytics such as the number of tool uses, general geographic region (country level), and browser type. This information is aggregated and cannot be linked to any individual user. This data is used solely to improve our service and understand which tools are most useful.

Cookies

FalPDF uses minimal, functional cookies only. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any cookies that profile your behaviour across other websites. The cookies we use are strictly necessary for the website to function (e.g., remembering your language preference).

Third-Party Services

FalPDF does not integrate with third-party advertising networks. We may use essential infrastructure services (cloud hosting) to operate our servers. These providers operate under strict data processing agreements and have no access to your file content.

Security

All file transfers between your browser and FalPDF servers are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Our servers are protected with industry-standard security practices. Given that we do not permanently store your files, the risk associated with data breaches is minimal by design.
